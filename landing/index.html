FAQ

What is Wormhole Name Service (WNS)?
WNS is a decentralized, cross-chain naming service that allows users to manage their identities across multiple blockchains with a single, user-friendly name.
How does WNS ensure security?
WNS incorporates advanced security features such as multi-signature, encryption, and decentralized storage to protect your digital identity.
Can I use WNS across different blockchains?
Yes, WNS is designed to be cross-chain compatible, allowing you to use your WNS identity on multiple supported blockchains.
What are the benefits of using WNS?
WNS provides a unified identity across multiple blockchains, simplified management, enhanced security, and seamless cross-chain transactions.
